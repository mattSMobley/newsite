COLORADO, UNITED STATES

Since the fall of 2015, I have lived in Fort Collins, Colorado. Fort Collins is 100% the coolest place I've ever had the pleasure of living in. As much as I like foreign travel, most of my free time is spent discovering Colorado. There is so much to offer: mountains, rivers, colorful rock formations, trails and parks, breweries, vineyards, etc. I grew up in Texas and was convinced Texas had the most beautiful sunsets, until I moved here (sorry friends and family in Texas). I mean, I'm running low on storage space on my phone just from taking so many sunset pictures.
