CRUISING

I've been on a handful of Caribbean cruises and two transatlantic cruises. I've always loved cruises, I think you get a lot for your money. But transatlantic cruises are another story. Both of the transatlantic cruises I've been on were under $500 per ticket. Not only is that less than the price of a one-way flight to Europe, but you also get to go to several amazing ports, get gourmet food, maid service, and a lot of time to relax/tan/work-out/read/etc.

PHILIPSBURG, SINT MAARTEN

The beaches in Philipsburg are the prettiest I've ever seen. April 2014.

FUNCHAL, MADEIRA

I would definitely go back to Funchal for a longer visit. It was a beautiful island city with a ton to offer. April 2015.

TENERIFE, CANARY ISLANDS

I wouldn't rate Tenerife as highly as Funchal, but it was still very nice. May 2014.

GIBRALTAR, U.K.

Gibraltar is an extremely cool spot for a one day trip. I wouldn't spend much more time than that, though. April 2015.

MALAGA, SPAIN

I've been to Malaga twice on transatlantic cruises and have seen entirely different things. Both times I absolutely loved it. May 2015 & April 2014.
