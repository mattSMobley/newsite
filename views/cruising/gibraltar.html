GIBRALTAR, U.K.

The city of Gibraltar is a litte bit rough around the edges, but the rock is a very entertaining attraction. The food is a mix of English, Spanish, and North African and most everyone speaks Spanish or English. The rock is the main reason people visit Gibraltar. I took a van tour of the rock, because it's quite a long way to walk on a street. It was definitely worth it. The guide told us a lot of history, showed us how to play with the apes, and took us to the best viewpoints.
