PORTUGAL

Portugal is one of the more underrated places in Europe. I would definitely put it in my top-5, maybe even top-3, favorite countries in which I've traveled. Everything is SUPER cheap, the cheapest in Western Europe. I mean like $5 for a salad/sandwich/wine in a high-traffic area, where tourists tend to get ripped off. Portugal has a very laid-back culture, kind of like Spain. In fact, in a lot of aspects, it reminded me of Spain. Only, I'd argue that Portugal has more attractions per square mile.
