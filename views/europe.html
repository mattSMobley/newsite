EUROPE

These are the European countries that I have visited. Whenever I visit a country, I try to spend AT LEAST a week there. It's important to me to spend time in the major cities, as well as some charming smaller cities. It really gives you a better idea of the country. Visit some sights/restaurants that you find highly-rated on TripAdvisor or Yelp(why not learn from those who came before you), and also visit some recommendations from locals. You may easily find a hidden gem. Be sure to try the local favorites as far as food and drinks!

SPAIN

Spain might be my favorite country in Europe. The prices, the sights, and the food make it really hard to beat. (3 cities featured)
