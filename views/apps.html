TRAVEL DESTINATION SUGGESTIONS

This app determines best travel destinations based on the month a user would like to vacation and the type of experience desired. After selecting your specific criteria, you'll be presented with a list of suggested destinations. Along with each city is a gallery of pictures and brief explanation.
The database of destinations was hand-created by me, after years of extensive travel research and my own travel experiences. There are currently around 70 destinations in the database, and I plan to keep adding more.



TRIPSTART

If you are curious about traveling to various cities, this is a perfect app for you. It brings in various pertinent info like weather, population, timezone/currency, a picture gallery, a point-of-interest map, and a terrain map.
