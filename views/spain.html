SPAIN

Spain is my favorite country to visit. The food and wine are arguably the best in Europe. It's about $2 for a glass of good wine and it usually comes with a small appetizer. There are flowers everywhere, beautiful churches and castles, beaches, cliffs, and a palable energy from the people. I have met so many nice people in Spain, it's easy to feel at home there.
