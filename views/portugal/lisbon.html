LISBON REGION, PORTUGAL

When I first got to Lisbon, I wasn't really impressed. It comes off a bit rougher than a lot of European cities. It quickly won me over, though. Lisbon had great food, was cheap, and very close to a lot of cool towns. But, the thing that I remember most fondly was how we met so many generous, friendly people. From Lisbon, I traveled to Cascais for a day(location of Praia do Guincho, or "James Bond Beach") and the amazing Sintra for a day.
