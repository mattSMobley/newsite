PORTO, PORTUGAL

Terri and I met a couple of friends from London in Porto for 4 days. We did some port wine tours, took cable cars and funiculars, walked to the coast and had fresh fish, and spent a lot of time down by the Douro river. Porto is a very underrated European destination. If you go here, you HAVE to try the francesinha, it is so delicious and horrible for you.
