SALO, SPAIN

My girlfriend and I spent almost a month doing helpx in Salo with a wonderful older couple. The town was quite small and there were some amazing views around.

More views from the house in Salo.

The couple we stayed with in Salo took us out every few days. Here we are pictured walking out of a nearby brewery. We stopped in to buy a beer but they didn't have a store or beer garden or anything. The owner gladly put together a sampler pack for 10 euros.

SOLSONNA

In this region, there were a surprising amount of small towns with excellent high-quality restaurants. This one was outside of Solsonna and featured some inspired, delicious dishes.

As I mentioned, many Catalonian towns identify much more with their region than their country. Solsonna is no different.
