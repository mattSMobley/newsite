ITALY

Unlike most of the countries in Europe, Italy did not quite meet my expectations. But that's not the entire story. One of the places I've visited in Italy, I've really liked(Padua), and one, is in my top favorite destinations that I've ever been(Cinque Terre). Just in general I had less-pleasant interactions, and noticed more trash and dishonesty in the 7 Italian cities I've visited, than in any other one country. Still, that's not to say it doesn't have it's amazing areas. Some of my favorite memories are from Italy. It can really have that effect on you sometimes.
