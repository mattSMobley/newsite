SOUTHERN FRANCE

Southern France is a very relaxing experience. It is all about 'ambiance' and conviviality. The pace of life is medium-slow and the people enjoy EVERYTHING outdoors. I spent around a month in Southern France in 2014 and experienced some of the best farmers markets and the best patio restaurants of anywhere I'd ever been. One thing that is not to be missed in Southern France, are the calanques - gorgeous white cliffs surrounding turquoise pools lined with (often nude) sunbathers, picnickers, and various adventurers.
